🌿 Who We Are

Rooted in Nature,
Built on Passion

FarmTrio was founded in 2023 with a simple but powerful vision — to provide Abeokuta and Ogun State with the highest quality, naturally raised livestock and farm produce. Located at the Farm Settlement Area, Ajebo Road Mile 6, Abeokuta, our farm is a sanctuary of sustainable agriculture.

🐐

Premium Livestock

Our goats and rams are raised free-range in a natural environment, ensuring healthy, quality animals for your family or business.

🐟

Aquaculture Excellence

Our fish ponds are maintained with clean, fresh water systems, producing healthy fish using environmentally responsible practices.

🌱

Sustainable Practices

We are committed to sustainable, eco-friendly farming that respects our land, animals, and the communities we serve across Ogun State.
